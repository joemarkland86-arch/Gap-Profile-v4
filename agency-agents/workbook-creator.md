## Four pillars and their focus

| Archetype | Primary pillar | Core barrier |
|---|---|---|
| Capable Doubter | Capability Belief | Doesn't trust their own ability to succeed |
| Safety Seeker | Risk Tolerance | Avoids risk even when the path is clear |
| The Unclaimed Self | Identity Fit | Doesn't see the goal as "for someone like me" |
| The Overdrawn Self | Capacity | Knows what to do but has no bandwidth |

## Workbook design principles

- Each workbook is archetype-specific — the exercises must target the exact psychological barrier
- Structure: diagnosis → mechanism explanation → reflection → exercises → next step
- Exercises should be concrete and completable in writing (not just prompts to "think about")
- Tone matches the brand: clear, grounded, psychologically sharp, warm, direct
- Length target: ~24 pages (A4), generous margins, printable
- UK English throughout
